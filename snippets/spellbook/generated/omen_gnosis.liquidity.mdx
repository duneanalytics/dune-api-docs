## omen_gnosis.liquidity 
A table containing all Omen liquidity management on Gnosis


<ParamField path="block_time">
Event UTC block time of each prediction market trade
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="block_day">
Event UTC block day of each prediction market trade
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_from">
Address which initiated the transaction
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_to">
Address which received the transaction
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_hash">
Unique transaction hash value tied to each transaction on the prediction market
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="evt_index">
Index of the log event in the block
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="fixedproductmarketmaker">
Address of the Fixed Product Market Maker
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="funder">
Address of the wallet adding or removing liquidity
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="shares">
Amount of shares involved in liquidity transactions
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="collateralremovedfromfeepool">
Collateral removed from the fee pool
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="outcomeindex">
Index of the outcome chosen
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="outcometokens_amount">
Amount of conditional tokens that were minted or burnt by the Fixed Product Market Maker
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="amount">
Amount in native currency that was bought or sold
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="action">
Action executed by the trader or market maker, &#x27;Buy&#x27;, &#x27;Sell&#x27;, &#x27;Add&#x27; and &#x27;Remove&#x27; 
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="reserves_delta">
Change in output token reserves
</ParamField>
