## governance_optimism.proposal_votes 
Events of votes for optimism governance proposals available on agora and snapshot platform 


<ParamField path="proposal_id">
The unique identifier of the proposal
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="proposal_link">
The link of the proposal on respective platform
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="platform">
The decentralized governance platform on which the proposal was created (e.g., Agora, Snapshot)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_hash">
The unique hash of voters voting transaction on proposal, tx_hash not available on snapshot proposals
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="date_timestamp">
The date timestamp when user voted on proposal
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="voter">
The unique voter address
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="votingWeightage">
The voting weight of the voter&#x27;s
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="choice">
Voter&#x27;s voting choice for the proposal
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="choice_name">
Voter&#x27;s voting choice&#x27;s name for the proposal
</ParamField>
