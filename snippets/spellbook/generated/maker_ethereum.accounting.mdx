## maker_ethereum.accounting 
Granular-level general ledger of MakerDAO with all accounting entries. Currently Work In Progress

<ParamField path="code">
Account of the accounting entry
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="ts">
Timestamp of the accounting entry
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="hash">
Transaction hash of the accounting entry, if any
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="value">
Value of the accounting entry, positive (credit) or negative (debit)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="token">
Token unit for the value
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="descriptor">
Label of the accounting entry
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="ilk">
Ilk that related to the accountring entry, if any
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="dai_value">
DAI Value of the accounting entry, positive (credit) or negative (debit)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="eth_value">
ETH Value of the accounting entry, positive (credit) or negative (debit)
</ParamField>
