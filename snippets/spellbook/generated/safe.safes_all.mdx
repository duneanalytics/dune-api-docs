## safe.safes_all 
Lists all Safes across chains.


<ParamField path="blockchain">
Blockchain on which the Safe is deployed
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="address">
Safe contract address
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="creation_version">
Initial version during creation
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="block_date">
Date of Safe creation
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="creation_time">
Datetime of Safe creation
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_hash">
Tx hash of Safe creation
</ParamField>
