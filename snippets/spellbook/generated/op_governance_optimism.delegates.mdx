## op_governance_optimism.delegates 
Tracks OP delegates voting power and delegating addresses data over time

<ParamField path="block_time">
Block time of transaction
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_hash">
Transaction hash
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="evt_index">
Event index
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="delegate">
Optimism addresses of delegates
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="current_voting_power">
Latest voting power of delegates
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="previous_voting_power">
Previous voting power of delegates
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="power_diff">
The difference between current_voting_power and previous_voting_power
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="voting_power_change">
Percentage change of delegates voting power over time
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="total_voting_power">
Total votable supply
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="voting_power_share">
Share of the total voting power expressed in percentage
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="number_of_delegators">
Number of addresses delegating voting power to the delegate
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="total_delegators">
Total number of addresses delegating voting power to delegates
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="total_delegators_share">
Share of the total delegating addresses expressed in percentage
</ParamField>
