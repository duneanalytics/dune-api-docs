## tessera_ethereum.fees 
Fees generated by Tessera&#x27;s Auctions. There are different types of auctions styles like LPDA.


<ParamField path="source">
Source of the fee. It could be &#x27;LPDA&#x27; for LPDA auctions.
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="vault">
Address of the Vault
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="amount">
Amount of the fee (ETH)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="receiver">
Address of the receiver of the fee
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="block_time">
Timestamp when the action took place
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_hash">
Transaction hash for the transaction in which the action took place
</ParamField>
