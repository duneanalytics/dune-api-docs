## gas_arbitrum.fees 
Gas Fees on Arbitrum rollup Layer 2


<ParamField path="blockchain">
Blockchain name
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="block_time">
Timestamp for block event time in UTC
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="block_number">
Block number
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_hash">
Primary key of the transaction
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_sender">
Initiated the transaction
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_receiver">
Received the transaction
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="native_token_symbol">
Symbol of native token (ETH) for the Ethereum Blockchain
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_amount_native">
Amount of native tokens (ETH) transferred from sender to recipient
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_amount_usd">
Amount of $USD transferred from sender to recipient
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_fee_native">
Total amount of native tokens (ETH) paid in gas fees
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_fee_usd">
Total amount of $USD paid in gas fees
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="burned_native">
Total amount of native tokens (ETH) burned for this transaction, not applicable to L2 because transactionsonly indirectly burn gas on L1
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="burned_usd">
Total amount of $USD burned for this transaction, not applicable to L2 because transactionsonly indirectly burn gas on L1
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="validator">
Address of blockchain validator for this transaction within the block, also referred to as miner before the Merge when Ethereum&#x27;s consensus mechanism was PoW
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gas_price_gwei">
Gas price (per gas unit) denoted in gwei
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gas_price_usd">
Gas price (per gas unit) denoted in $USD
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gas_price_bid_gwei">
Gas price (per gas unit) estimated by ArbOs with a buffer denoted in gwei. All excess is refunded
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gas_price_bid_usd">
Gas price (per gas unit) estimated by ArbOs with a buffer denoted in $USD. All excess is refunded
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gas_used">
Amount of L2 gas units consumed by the transaction
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gas_limit">
Maximum amount of gas units that can be consumed by the transaction on Optimism L2
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gas_usage_percent">
Percentage of Gas Used relative to the gas limit on Optimism L2
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="l1_gas_used">
Amount of L1 gas units consumed by the transaction
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="type">
Transaction type: Legacy (Pre EIP 1559) or DynamicFee (Post EIP-1559)
</ParamField>
