## yield_yak_avalanche_c.balances 
yield yak vault/farm/pool balances on avalanche_c as a temporal table


<ParamField path="blockchain">
Blockchain on which the YRT contract is deployed
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="contract_address">
Address of the YRT smart contract / strategy
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="from_time">
UTC time from which the contract held this balance in its respective deposit token
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="to_time">
UTC time until which the contract held this balance in its respective deposit token
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="deposit_token_balance">
Balance of deposit tokens held in the contract in the time window specified
</ParamField>
