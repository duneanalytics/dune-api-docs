## ethereum.blobs 
Blob table but enriched with size, blob version hash, blob submitter, base fee, ..

<ParamField path="beacon_slot_time">
UTC slot time of the beacon slot
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="blob_versioned_hash">
hash of the blob
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="beacon_epoch">
A specific epoch during which this block was proposed. An epoch is a division of time in Ethereum&#x27;s Proof of Stake mechanism.
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="beacon_slot_number">
A slot in which a validator has the opportunity to propose a new block. Each slot represents a chance for a block to be added.
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="blob_index">
Index of the blob in the block slot
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="beacon_proposer_index">
Identifier for the validator that proposed the specific block.
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="blob_kzg_commitment">
A cryptographic commitment used to ensure data integrity within blob transactions. KZG commitments are a form of cryptographic proof used to represent blob data within blocks.
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="blob_parent_root">
A hash of the parent block, linking the current block to the previous one in the blockchain.
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="used_blob_byte_count">
total non-zero byte size of the blob
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="blob_byte_count">
total byte size of the blob
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="blob_gas_used">
gas units used by the blob
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="blob_submitter">
address that submitted the blob
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="blob_submitter_label">
the entity that submitted the blob
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="blob_base_fee">
the base fee (wei) per blob gas
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="max_fee_per_blob_gas">
the maximum fee (wei) per blob gas the tx is willing to pay
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="is_blobscription">
boolean wether the blob was used as a blobscription
</ParamField>
