## balancer_ethereum.vebal_slopes 
Slope and bias of veBAL per wallet after each balance update Depends on veBAL_call_create_lock, veBAL_evt_Deposit and veBAL_evt_Withdraw


<ParamField path="block_number">
Block number
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="block_time">
Block time in UTC
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="block_timestamp">
Block timestamp
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="wallet_address">
Address of the wallet holding the veBAL
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="bpt_balance">
Amount of BPT held in the veBAL lock
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="unlocked_at">
Timestamp at which B-80BAL-20WETH BPT can be unlocked
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="slope">
veBAL decay rate (per second)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="bias">
veBAL balance at the moment user locks or re-locks
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="block_date">
Block time trunc to date
</ParamField>
