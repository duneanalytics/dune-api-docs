## balancer_ethereum.vebal_balances_day 
Daily balances of veBAL per wallet Depends on veBAL_call_create_lock, veBAL_evt_Deposit and veBAL_evt_Withdraw


<ParamField path="day">
UTC event block time truncated to the day mark
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="wallet_address">
Address of the wallet holding the veBAL
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="bpt_balance">
Amount of BPT held in the veBAL lock
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="vebal_balance">
Amount of veBAL
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="lock_time">
Amount of time the BPT was locked for at the last time the lock was updated
</ParamField>
