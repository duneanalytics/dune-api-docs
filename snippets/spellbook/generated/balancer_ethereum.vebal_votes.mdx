## balancer_ethereum.vebal_votes 
Records of votes for Balancer gauges by provider at each voting round Depends on GaugeController_evt_VoteForGauge and the vebal_slopes spell


<ParamField path="round_id">
ID of the voting round
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="start_date">
Day of the voting round start date
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="end_date">
Day of the voting round end date
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gauge">
Address of the gauge which provider votes for
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="provider">
Address of the wallet which voted for the gauge
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="vote">
Weight of the vote performed by the provider for the gauge
</ParamField>
