## nft_ethereum.wallet_metrics 
This spell aggregates nft trading stats (profit, ROI, spend, gain, loss/win %, collections traded) per wallet and filters out wash trades.


<ParamField path="wallet">
NFT trader&#x27;s wallet address
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="unique_collections_traded">
Count of unqiue collections/ERC721-contracts traded
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="buys_count">
Count of NFT buys or mints
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="sells_count">
Count of NFT sales
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="trades_count">
Count of Mints+Buys+Sells
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="profitable_trades_count">
Count of trades that were protitable (i.e. profit &gt; 0)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="unprofitable_trades_count">
Count of trades that were unprotitable (i.e. profit &lt;&#x3D; 0)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="win_percentage">
Percent of sales that had positive profit
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="loss_percentage">
Percent of sales that had negative profit
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="breakeven_percentage">
Percent of sales that had breakeven profit
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="spent_eth">
Amount of ETH spent on NFTs (not including gas)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="spent_eth_realized">
Amount of ETH spent on NFTs that were later sold
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="spent_eth_unrealized">
Amount of ETH spent on NFTs that have not been sold yet
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gained_eth">
Amount of ETH gained from NFTs sold or appreication of currently held NFTs based on floor price
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gained_eth_realized">
Amount of ETH gained from NFTs sold
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gained_eth_unrealized">
Amount of ETH gained from appreication of currently held NFTs based on floor price
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="roi_eth">
Percent ETH ROI from appreciation+sales over buy amount (range 0-1)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="roi_eth_realized">
Percent ETH ROI from sales over buy amount on those sales (range 0-1)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="roi_eth_unrealized">
Percent ETH ROI from appreciation over buy amount on unsold NFTs (range 0-1)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="eth_profit">
Total of realized + unrealized profit from NFTs (i.e. (sale_price-buy_price) for sold nfts + (current_floor_price - buy_price) for unsold NFTs)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="eth_profit_realized">
 Total of realized profit (in ETH) from sale of NFTs
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="eth_profit_unrealized">
Total of unrealized profit (in ETH) from sale of NFTs
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="avg_win_size">
Average size of profit from protitable NFT trades
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="avg_loss_size">
Average size of loss from protitable NFT trades
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="unique_weeks_buying">
Unique weeks the trader bought NFTs in
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="unique_weeks_selling">
Unique weeks the trader sold NFTs in
</ParamField>
