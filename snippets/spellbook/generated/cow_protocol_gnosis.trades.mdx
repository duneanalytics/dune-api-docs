## cow_protocol_gnosis.trades 
CoW Protocol enriched trades list on Gnosis Chain


<ParamField path="block_date">
UTC event block date of each trade
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="block_time">
Timestamp for block event time in UTC
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_hash">
Transaction hash of trade
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="evt_index">
Index of the corresponding trade event
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="order_uid">
Unique identifier of order involved in trade. Note that partially fillable orders can be touched multiple times so this is not a unique ID for trade events.
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="trader">
Owner of the order being traded (aka trader)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="sell_token_address">
Ethereum address of sellToken
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="sell_token">
Symbol of sellToken
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="buy_token_address">
Ethereum address of buyToken
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="buy_token">
Symbol of buyToken
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="token_pair">
Ordered concatenation of buy/sell token symbols involved in trade
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="units_sold">
Units of sellToken sold (incorporates token decimals).
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="atoms_sold">
Amount (in atoms) of the sellToken sold
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="units_bought">
Units of buyToken bought (incorporates token decimals).
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="atoms_bought">
Amount (in atoms) of the buyToken bought
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="usd_value">
USD value of trade (taken as the max of buy value, sell value when both available, otherwise whichever is known)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="buy_price">
USD price of buyToken at the time of trade
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="buy_value_usd">
USD value of buyToken amount
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="sell_price">
USD price of sellToken at the time of trade
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="sell_value_usd">
USD value of sellToken amount
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="fee">
Unit of fee taken (in sellToken) - incorporating token decimals
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="fee_atoms">
Amount (in atoms) of fee - taken in sellToken
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="fee_usd">
USD value of trade fee
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="app_data">
Hashed metadata related to trade event (full content available on IPFS)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="receiver">
Recipient of trades buyToken
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="limit_sell_amount">
Sell amount side of the limit price
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="limit_buy_amount">
Buy amount side of the limit price
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="valid_to">
epoch timestamp (in seconds) until when the order was valid
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="flags">
bitmap with additional trade information (cf. https://github.com/cowprotocol/contracts/blob/main/src/contracts/libraries/GPv2Trade.sol#L58-L94)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="surplus_usd">
USD value of surplus (difference between limit and executed price)
</ParamField>
