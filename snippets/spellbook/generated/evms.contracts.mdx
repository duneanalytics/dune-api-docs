## evms.contracts 
A view keeping track of what contracts are decoded across EVM chains on Dune; contains information associated with the decoded contract such as namespace, name, address, ABI.

<ParamField path="blockchain">
Name of the blockchain
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="abi">
ABI of the decoded contract
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="address">
Address of the decoded contract
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="from">
Address that created/deployed this contract
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="code">
Code for contract creation
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="name">
Name of the decoded contract
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="namespace">
Namesapce of the decoded contract
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="dynamic">
Boolean indicating whether the contract is dynamic or not
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="base">
Boolean indicating whether the contract is the base contract or not
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="factory">
Boolean indicating whether the contract is a factory contract or not
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="detection_source">
Detection source: &#x27;factory&#x27;, &#x27;base&#x27;, or &#x27;dynamic&#x27;
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="created_at">
Timestamp for contract creation
</ParamField>
