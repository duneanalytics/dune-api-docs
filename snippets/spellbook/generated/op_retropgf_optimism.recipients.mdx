## op_retropgf_optimism.recipients 
Data from onchain attestations about Optimism Retroactive Public Goods Funding (RetroPGF) Recipients

<ParamField path="blockchain">
Chain where the RetroPGF Round took place
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="round_name">
Name of the RetroPGF Round
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="block_date">
Date of the first attestation
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="submitter_address">
Address of the recipient of the attestation (and form submitter address)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="issuer">
Address issuing the attestation
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="recipient_name">
Submitted name of the recipient
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="recipient_category">
Submitted name of the recipient
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="award_amount">
Amount of funding received
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="award_token">
Token that the funding was paid out in
</ParamField>
