## op_retropgf_optimism.voters 
Data from onchain attestations about Optimism Retroactive Public Goods Funding (RetroPGF) Voters

<ParamField path="blockchain">
Chain where the RetroPGF Round took place
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="round_name">
Name of the RetroPGF Round
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="block_date">
Date of the first attestation
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="voter">
Address of the recipient of the attestation (the voter)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="issuer">
Address issuing the attestation
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="can_vote">
Is the voter address eligible to vote (true/false)
</ParamField>
