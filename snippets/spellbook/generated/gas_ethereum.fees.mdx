## gas_ethereum.fees 
Gas Fees on Ethereum


<ParamField path="blockchain">
Blockchain name
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="block_time">
Timestamp for block event time in UTC
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="block_number">
Block number
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_hash">
Primary key of the transaction
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_sender">
Initiated the transaction
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_receiver">
Received the transaction
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="native_token_symbol">
Symbol of native token (ETH) for the Ethereum Blockchain
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_amount_native">
Amount of native tokens (ETH) transferred from sender to recipient
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_amount_usd">
Amount of $USD transferred from sender to recipient
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_fee_native">
Total amount of native tokens (ETH) paid in gas fees -- Pre EIP 1559: Gas Price * Gas Used; Post EIP 1559: Gas Price * Gas Used ; Base fee + Priority fee) * Gas used
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_fee_usd">
Total amount of $USD paid in gas fees
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="burned_native">
Total amount of native tokens (ETH) burned for this transaction, Post EIP 1559: Is equal to the Base Fee
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="burned_usd">
Total amount of $USD burned for this transaction
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_savings_native">
Total amount of native tokens (ETH) refunded to the user if the max fee exceeded the total transaction fee after the transaction execution
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="tx_savings_usd">
Total amount of $USD refunded to the user
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="validator">
Address of blockchain validator for this transaction within the block, also referred to as miner before the Merge when Ethereum&#x27;s consensus mechanism was PoW
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="max_fee_gwei">
Maximum amount of gas (in Gwei) willing to be paid for the transaction: Is refunded if exceeds the total transaction fee once the transaction is executed
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="max_fee_usd">
Maximum amount of gas (in $USD) willing to be paid for the transaction: Is refunded if exceeds the total transaction fee once the transaction is executed
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="base_fee_gwei">
Market price for gas (in Gwei)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="base_fee_usd">
Market price for gas (in $USD)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="priority_fee_gwei">
Maximum amount of gas (in Gwei) to be included as a tip to the miner/validator
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="priority_fee_usd">
Maximum amount of gas (in $USD) to be included as a tip to the miner/validator
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gas_price_gwei">
Gas price (per gas unit) denoted in gwei
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gas_price_usd">
Gas price (per gas unit) denoted in $USD
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gas_used">
Amount of gas units consumed by the transaction
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gas_limit">
Maximum amount of gas units that can be consumed by the transaction
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="gas_usage_percent">
Percentage of Gas Used relative to the gas limit
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="difficulty">
Maximum amount of gas units that can be consumed by the transaction
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="type">
Transaction type: Legacy (Pre EIP 1559) or DynamicFee (Post EIP-1559)
</ParamField>
