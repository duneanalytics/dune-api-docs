## sushiswap_optimism.pool_incentives_config 
Sushiswap contract deployment constuctor configurations for rewarder contracts


<ParamField path="blockchain">
Blockchain which the DEX is deployed
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="contract_address">
Address of the contract observed
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="reward_token">
Address of the token used for rewards
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="reward_per_second">
Total rewards issues per second (not decimal adjusted)
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="masterchef_v2_address">
Address of the corresponding Masterchef staking contract
</ParamField>
