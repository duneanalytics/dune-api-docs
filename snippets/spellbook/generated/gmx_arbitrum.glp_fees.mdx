View GLP fee accrued in the Fee GLP contract.

UTC event block time truncated to the minute
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="block_date">
UTC event block time truncated to the date
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="fees_weth_generated">
Fees generated in WETH
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="fees_weth_cumulative">
Cumulative accrued fees in WETH
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="weth_current_price">
Current price of WETH as calculated per the GMX smart contract
</ParamField>
