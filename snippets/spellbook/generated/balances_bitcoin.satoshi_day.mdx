## balances_bitcoin.satoshi_day 
Daily token balances of Satoshi Bitcoin per wallets


<ParamField path="blockchain">
Chai name
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="day">
UTC event block time truncated to the day mark
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="wallet_address">
Wallet address of sender or receiver. If amount is negative, wallet address is the sender&#x27;s.
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="amount_raw">
The number of Satoshis in the wallet
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="amount">
The number of Bitcoins in the wallet
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="price_btc">
Price of btc at day
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="profit">
Profit by USD of the wallet
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="amount_usd">
USD balance of the wallet
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="total_asset">
Total asset by USD of the wallet
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="updated_at">
When the balances were last updated
</ParamField>
